# 🔬 Advanced Scientific Calculator

A comprehensive, all-in-one scientific calculator built in C++ that handles everything from basic arithmetic to complex calculus operations. Perfect for students, engineers, and professionals who need a powerful calculation tool right in their terminal.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧮 Core & Advanced Operations

#### Basic Arithmetic & Algebra
- Standard arithmetic operations (+, -, ×, ÷)
- Fraction calculations (improper and mixed fractions)
- Algebraic expressions handling

#### Powers & Roots
- Squares (x²) and cubes (x³)
- Square roots (√) and cube roots (∛)
- nth roots and reciprocals (1/x)
- General exponentiation (x^y)

#### Equation Solving
- **Linear equations** (ax + b = 0)
- **Quadratic equations** (ax² + bx + c = 0)
- **Cubic equations** (ax³ + bx² + cx + d = 0)
- **Inequalities** (<, >, ≤, ≥) with interval solutions

#### Scientific Constants & Conversions
- **Built-in constants**: π (Pi), e (Euler), g (Gravity), c (Light speed), h (Planck), R (Gas constant), NA (Avogadro), k (Boltzmann)
- **Unit conversions**:
  - Length: meters ↔ feet, kilometers ↔ miles
  - Mass: kilograms ↔ pounds
  - Temperature: Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit
  - Volume: liters ↔ gallons

---

### 📐 Calculus & Higher Mathematics

#### Calculus Operations
- **Numerical differentiation** (first derivative approximation)
- **Numerical integration** (Simpson's rule)
- Custom function support (f(x) = x² + 2x + 1)

#### Matrix & Vector Operations
- **Matrices** (up to 4×4):
  - Addition, subtraction, multiplication
  - Display formatted output
- **Vectors** (2D & 3D):
  - Dot product and cross product
  - Angle between vectors
  - Magnitude calculation

#### Complex Numbers
- Operations in rectangular form (a + bi):
  - Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
  - Magnitude (modulus)
  - Complex conjugate
- Support for polar form representation

---

### 📊 Trigonometry & Functions

#### Trigonometric Functions
- **Basic**: sin, cos, tan (degrees and radians)
- **Inverse**: arcsin, arccos, arctan
- **Hyperbolic**: sinh, cosh, tanh and their inverses

#### Logarithms
- Natural logarithms (ln)
- Common logarithms (log₁₀)
- Custom base logarithms (logₐb)

---

### 📈 Statistics & Probability

#### Statistical Analysis
- **One-variable statistics**:
  - Mean, variance, standard deviation
  - Data point analysis
- **Two-variable regression**:
  - Linear regression (y = mx + b)
  - Correlation analysis

#### Probability & Combinatorics
- Permutations (nPr)
- Combinations (nCr)
- Random number generator (custom range)

---

### 🔢 Advanced Features

#### Number Systems
- **Base conversions** between:
  - Binary (base-2)
  - Octal (base-8)
  - Decimal (base-10)
  - Hexadecimal (base-16)
- Support for both forward and reverse conversions

#### Summation Calculator
- Sum of integers over a range
- Sum of squares (Σn²)
- Sum of cubes (Σn³)

#### Spreadsheet Module
- **5×5 interactive spreadsheet**
- Cell editing and value storage
- Row/column summation
- Row average calculations

#### QR Code Generator
- ASCII art QR code generation
- Text encoding (max 20 characters)
- Visual representation for smartphone scanning
